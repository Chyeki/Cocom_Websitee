P Words
  • Parsimonious

    • Parsimonious means excessively unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy or frugal.

    • Despite his wealth, he was known for his parsimonious lifestyle, never spending money unnecessarily.
  • Peregrinate

    • Peregrinate means to travel or wander around from place to place, especially on foot.

    • After retiring, they decided to peregrinate around the world, visiting every continent.
  • Penumbra

    • Penumbra refers to the partial shadow that surrounds the complete shadow of an opaque object, like during an eclipse.

    • During the eclipse, the area outside the shadow's center experienced a penumbra, where the sun was only partially obscured.
  • Perspicacious

    • Perspicacious means having a keen mental perception or understanding; discerning.

    • The detective was incredibly perspicacious, noticing small details that others often overlooked.
  • Pulchritude

    • Pulchritude refers to physical beauty, especially in a woman.

    • The painting captured the pulchritude of the natural landscape, with vibrant colors and stunning detail.
