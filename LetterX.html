X Words
  • Xenial

    • Relating to hospitality, especially towards strangers or guests.

    • The xenial host welcomed us into his home with warmth and generosity.
  • Xeric

    • Characterized by or adapted to a dry environment or climate.

    • Cacti and succulents have xeric adaptations that help them survive in arid regions.
  • Xenophobia

    • Fear or hatred of foreigners or strangers.

    • Xenophobia can lead to discrimination and hostility towards individuals from different cultural backgrounds.
  • Xerophyte

    • A plant adapted to survive in a dry or arid environment, typically by storing water or reducing water loss.

    • Cacti and other desert plants are examples of xerophytes.
  • Xylophagous

    • Feeding on wood or woody tissue, often used to describe insects or organisms that consume wood.

    • Termites are xylophagous insects that can cause significant damage to wooden structures.
