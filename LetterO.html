O Words
  • Obfuscate

    • Obfuscate means to deliberately make something unclear or confusing.

    • The company's financial report was intentionally obfuscated to hide its true losses.
  • Osculate

    • Osculate means to come into close contact or to touch lightly.

    • The leaves of the trees seemed to osculate gently in the breeze.
  • Obstreperous

    • Obstreperous refers to being noisy, unruly, or difficult to control.

    • The classroom became quite obstreperous when the substitute teacher arrived.
  • Orotund

    • Orotund describes a strong, resonant, and impressive voice.

    • The actor's voice was deep and orotund, commanding the attention of the entire audience.
  • Ossify

    • Ossify means to become hardened or rigid, often referring to tissue turning into bone.

    • Over time, the cartilage in the body can ossify and turn into bone.
